  • Jacklyn Wei

    Founder, CEO, Chairwoman

    Jacklyn Wei is a chess lover with 9 years of competitive chess experience. She is a 2x state champion and the 2023 Utah Girls State Champion, representing Utah at the national tournament of champions and placing in the Top 20 nationally. In her free time, Jacklyn enjoys playing tennis, drawing, listening to K-Pop, and socializing.

  • Brian Wei

    Vice President, CTO

    Brian Wei always had a deep interest in chess since 3rd grade, when he ranked 11th in his very first tournament by executing only the 4-move checkmate. Since then, he has become the state champion and has consistently placed in the top three for multiple years. Outside of chess, Brian loves coding, teaching, math, and music.

  • Olivia Jiang

    Role

    Olivia Jiang has been a passionate chess player since 1st grade. She is a 3-time state champion and has been the Utah’s Women’s State Champion for 2 consecutive years (2022 & 2023). She has also been consistently ranked in the Top Player Lists by USCF for many years. Other hobbies she enjoys include piano, math, tennis, and art.

  • Shreya Sanghvi
